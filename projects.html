Joint Fire Sciences Program: Oregon Wildfire Policy

A policy implementation analysis of managing wildfire risk in Oregon. Click the link above to learn more.

Summary: Since 2021, Oregon has been rolling out and revising its wildfire codes and standards, informed by a new statewide hazard map. This project seeks to better understand ways in which the implementation of Oregon's SB 762/80 map (and associated policy) could be more inclusive, effective, and equitable. The results of this Joint Fire Science Program-funded research will culminate in a summary guide for policymakers in Oregon and other states looking to implement similar legislation.
